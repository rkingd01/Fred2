Better days

Those who escaped "Czech Hell" walked for days in order to surrender to American forces.

It is ironic that Czech partisans executed hundreds of Estonians because of the SS insignia yet the US forces found them to be untainted by any atrocities and even selected soldiers from the Estonian-SS Legion to guard German prisoners awaiting their trial at Nuremburg.

In Kent, life changed for the better. Working on the farm gave him and other Estonians a regular income and a secure home. They socialised as much as possible.
